1. Overview

  • ClassSeats does not operate any backend servers.
  • ClassSeats does not sell, share, or monetize user data.
  • All classroom data is stored locally on your device and/or in your own Google Drive, depending on your chosen settings.
  • Student data is not transmitted to ClassSeats or any third party.

2. Information ClassSeats Accesses

A. Google Account Information

If you choose to connect Google Drive, ClassSeats requests limited access to:

  • Your Google account email address (used only to identify the signed-in user)
  • Files created or explicitly selected by ClassSeats in your Google Drive

ClassSeats uses the Google Drive drive.file scope, which allows access only to files the app creates or that you explicitly open using the app. ClassSeats cannot view, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive.

B. Classroom Data

Classroom data may include:

  • Student names
  • Seating arrangements
  • Attendance records
  • Group assignments
  • Teacher-entered notes or alerts

This data is stored locally on your device and/or stored in files saved to your own Google Drive. ClassSeats does not store this data on external servers and does not have access to it.

3. How Information Is Used

ClassSeats uses data only to provide core application features, including:

  • Saving and loading classroom files
  • Synchronizing files between your devices (when Google Drive is enabled)
  • Displaying your data within the app

Data is never used for advertising, analytics profiling, or marketing.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local data is stored on your device using standard browser or application storage mechanisms.
  • Google Drive data is stored entirely within your own Google Drive account.
  • ClassSeats does not maintain copies of your files.

Because data remains under your control, you may delete, export, or back up your files at any time using standard device or Google Drive tools.

5. Data Sharing

ClassSeats does not share data with advertisers, third-party vendors, or analytics platforms that access classroom data.

Data is shared only when you explicitly choose to share files from your own Google Drive.
