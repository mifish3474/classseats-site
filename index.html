How Google Drive sync works

  1. You choose Drive sync. ClassSeats works with local storage by default. Drive is optional.
  2. You sign in to Google. ClassSeats requests the limited drive.file permission.
  3. You save ClassSeats files to Drive. Only files created by ClassSeats (or you explicitly open with ClassSeats) are accessible.
  4. You can disconnect anytime. Disconnecting stops Drive access; the app continues using local storage.
